  • About Us

    We are Sunpack

    Established in the year 1994, we Sun Pack are reputed Manufacturer and Supplier of comprehensive array of Thermocol Packaging Materials, Corrugated Packaging Materials, Duplex Boxes and Air Bubble Sheet And Rolls. Our offered products are widely used in various industries for packaging products. Located at Noida (Uttar Pradesh, India), we backed by robust infrastructure that spread over a vast area. For streamline manufacturing process, we have segregated our infrastructural base into units like procuring, manufacturing, quality testing, sales & marketing, warehousing & packaging and logistic. Our manufacturing unit is equipped with modern machinery and tools that assist us in manufacturing products effectively. Under the headship of our Proprietor Mr. Rohit Sachdeva, we have gained indomitable position in the market.
